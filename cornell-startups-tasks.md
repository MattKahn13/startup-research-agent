# Cornell Alumni + Startups Research Tasks

## Context

Current approach to finding Cornell alumni + startups relies on LinkedIn data, which misses:
- Startups where the founder doesn't have a LinkedIn page
- Some cases even where the founder *does* have a LinkedIn page

Large companies are particularly affected — e.g., Citigroup (founded by Sandy Weill, no LI page) has ~250,000 employees alone, which would push the total over 2MM.

## Tasks

### 1. Cover the "High Profile Cornell Startups" list
- **Source:** https://eship.cornell.edu/cornell-startups/high-profile-startups/
- **Goal:** Add every company on this list to the dataset, including large/legacy companies whose founders are not on LinkedIn
- **Why it matters:** This list captures big companies (like Citigroup) that the LinkedIn-based pipeline currently misses

### 2. Cross-reference with Tony Chen's "Big Red Startups" list
- **Source:** https://bigredai.org/startups
- **Goal:** Add companies from this list to the dataset
- **Caveat:** Accuracy of this list is unverified — flag entries that look questionable for review

## Deadline

Ideally completed before **5/15** (return from trip).
